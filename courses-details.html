What we Do

We deal in University Applications, Visa Processing and Job Applications.

Overview

University Applications

The following are required for university applications:

  • Academic papers (KCSE, University Degree)
  • Personal statement
  • Birth certificate
  • Traveling passport
  • 3 options of the courses you want
Visa Applications

The following are required for university applications:

  • Valid passport copy
  • Passport photo-plain white background (35mm x 45mm) Digital photo
  • Applicants ID
  • Applicants Birth Certificate
  • Applicants Personal email and address
  • Current updated resume
  • Marriage certificate or affidavit
  • Previous travel history (visa, receipts)
  • School Details (high school, college, university, tertiary)
  • Previous Employment recommendation letter
  • Current Job offer & Verification letter
  • 4 months pay slips
  • Financial statement (bank statement for 6 months) i.e. Personal, Business or Company
  • Birth certificate for the children
  • Spouse ID copies
  • Business documents (Business registration certificate, permit, CR12, picture of work place, KRA pin, contacts, TCC)
  • Proof of any assets i.e. (Logbook, title deed, insurance policy)
  • Parents ID copies
  • Covid Vaccination Certificate
  • Good Conduct
  • Travel Insurance
